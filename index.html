About Jurate

I am a beauty professional based in Waterford, Ireland, where I work at Beauty Salon Name. For me, this work is about more than nails or make-up—it’s about creating a calm space where you can relax and leave feeling a little brighter.

About a year ago I was diagnosed with breast cancer. I went through treatment and, with the support of my family and medical team, I came through it. I’m still on medication and taking care of my health, but I’m back doing what I love. That experience deepened my belief in self-care, in looking after your body and mind, and in the importance of feeling good in your own skin. I bring that perspective into every appointment.

When you sit in my chair, you get my full attention: quality work, a clean environment and a friendly, no-fuss atmosphere. I’d love to welcome you.

My approach

I believe in comfort, cleanliness and really listening to what you want. My goal is to enhance your natural beauty with care and attention to detail—no pressure, no drama. I value honest, friendly communication and a calm, positive atmosphere. I’m not into gossip; I’d rather we focus on you and your treatment.

I want every client to leave feeling a little more beautiful and a little more relaxed, even when life is busy.

Beyond the salon

When I’m not at the salon, I’m a mom of two: my daughter Kameja and my son Jonas. Juggling work and family keeps me on my toes, but I wouldn’t have it any other way. When my routine allows it, I love to travel—it’s my way to recharge and see a bit of the world.
